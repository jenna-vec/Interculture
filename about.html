INTERCULTURE - DIVING INTO MULTICULTURALISM

At what point does cultural immersion cross into identity loss? Do interfaith relationships work? Friends Jenna and Ibrahim discuss a different topic within the fascinating, and often weird world of multiculturalism. They get personal while discussing the challenges of immigration, intercultural relationships, and shifting identity.

Jenna is writer and designer, and not above drinking shitty coffee.

Ibrahim is a student in Business Administration and a fan of fast cars he can’t afford.
